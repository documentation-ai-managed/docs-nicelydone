## Save Your Favorites

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in to your account">
    Sign in at nicelydone.app for saving capability.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Browse or search for inspiration">
    Use search and filters to find a screen or flow you like.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click the heart icon">
    On any screen, click the heart at top right to add to Favorites.
  </Step>
  <Step title="View your saved items">
    Go to the "Favorites" tab in your profile to revisit saved designs.
  </Step>
</Steps>
