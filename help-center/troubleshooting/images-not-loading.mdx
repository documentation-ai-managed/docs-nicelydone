---
title: Images Not Loading
description: Troubleshoot why Nicelydone images aren't displaying.
---

## Problem

- Screen or flow images don't appear
- Placeholders or broken images shown

## Quick Fix

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check your internet connection">
    Reload Nicelydone or try opening a different website to confirm connectivity.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Disable ad blockers">
    Some extensions may block images. Temporarily disable and refresh the page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Clear browser cache">
    Outdated cache can cause loading issues. Clear and reload Nicelydone.
  </Step>
</Steps>
