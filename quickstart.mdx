## Create Your Nicelydone Account

Follow these steps to sign up quickly.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit Nicelydone" icon="globe">
    Open your browser and navigate to [nicelydone.com](https://nicelydone.com).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Sign Up" icon="user-plus">
    Locate the prominent `Sign Up` button in the top-right corner and click it.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Details" icon="edit-3">
    Provide your email, create a password, and optionally add your name. Accept the terms and click `Create Account`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify Email" icon="mail">
    Check your inbox for a verification email from Nicelydone and click the link to activate your account.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Access the Main Dashboard

Once verified, log in to explore 178,400+ screens and 7,900+ user flows.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log In" icon="log-in">
    Return to [nicelydone.com](https://nicelydone.com) and click `Log In`. Enter your credentials.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Dashboard Loads" icon="layout-dashboard">
    You'll land on the main dashboard, featuring search bar, recent inspirations, and category filters.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout kind="tip">
  Bookmark `https://nicelydone.com/dashboard` for quick access next time.
</Callout>

## Perform a Basic Screen Search

Search for design inspirations across top SaaS apps.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Screens" icon="image">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Enter Query" icon="search">
        In the dashboard search bar, type `"login modal"` and hit Enter.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Browse Results" icon="grid">
        Scroll through thumbnails from apps like Slack, Notion, and Figma. Click any to view full screen details.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Save Favorite" icon="heart">
        Hover over a screen and click the heart icon to add it to your collections.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="User Flows" icon="git-branch">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Search Flows" icon="search">
        Type `"onboarding flow"` in the search bar and filter by `User Flows`.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Explore Steps" icon="arrow-right">
        Click a flow to see the interactive sequence of screens, with transitions and annotations.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>
